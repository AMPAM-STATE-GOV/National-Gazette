# AMPAM National Gazette
## Official Public Record of the Allodium Moroccan Praedium Ante Michigan

This repository serves as the official international public notice forum for AMPAM. All legislation, executive decrees, ministerial orders, consular court judgments, and public notices are published here as the official record of the nation.

**Authority Chain:** Madrid Protocol 1880 → AMPAM → ADDIF Registry

**Treaty Basis:** Treaty of Peace and Friendship 1786/1836 | Madrid Protocol 1880 | General Act of Algeciras 1906
